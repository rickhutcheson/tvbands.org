Amanda Blevins | Director of Bands

Amanda G. Blevins, in her 21st year of teaching, began her career at Bishop Watterson High School in Columbus, Ohio.  Under her direction at Tri-Valley, the Marching Scotties, Symphonic, and Junior High Bands have consistently obtained Superior and Excellent Ratings at OMEA District and State Marching Competitions and OMEA District Concert Band Contest.  The Marching Scotties have competed nationally in the Bands of American competition, at Disney World in Florida, as well as performing for several Longaberger and Community events. 

Ms. Blevins is currently the Advocacy Committee Chair for the Ohio Music Education Association.Â  She has been a performing member of the Heisey Wind Ensemble at the Ohio State University-Newark.  She has served as a writer for the Ohio Fine Arts Curriculum Model Team.  In the past she has worked with other music and arts teachers to develop the Muskingum County Fine Arts Course of Study.  Ms. Blevins is currently a National Board Certified Teacher for music education. In 2007 Ms. Blevins was the recipient of the Daybreak Rotary Teacher of Excellence and in 2011 the Tri-Valley Teacher of the Year.

In addition to providing instruction in music, Ms. Blevins incorporates multicultural, integrated arts into the curriculum.  The 2016 "Oceans, Rivers, and Seas" program showcased music, literature, art, science and historical projects based on different repertoire.
